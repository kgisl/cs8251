

## stringCat

Write a pointer version of the function`strcat`  that  `strcat(s,t)`  copies the string`t`  to the end of  `s`  .

### Example

**input**  
`abc` `de`  
**output**  
`abcde`

**input**  
`hello` `world`  
**output**  
`helloworld`
